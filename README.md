**LearnX** is an AI-powered platform that helps high school students explore careers, discover opportunities, and build structured learning roadmaps tailored to their interests and strengths.

---

## 🌟 Overview

Students today face:
- Limited access to personalized counseling  
- Thousands of overwhelming career and college paths  
- Unreliable or scattered online information  
- Difficulty using AI tools effectively  

**LearnX solves this** by combining career discovery, opportunity matching, and guided skill-building into one unified platform.

---

## 🧠 Features

### 🔍 Personalized Career & Major Discovery
- Input hobbies, extracurriculars, courses, and strengths  
- Get tailored career paths and college major suggestions  
- Understand *why* each path fits you  

---

### 🎯 Opportunity Recommendations
- Curated:
  - Online courses  
  - Competitions  
  - Summer programs  
  - College application tips  
- Personalized to your selected path  

---

### 📅 AI-Generated 4-Week Roadmaps
- Structured weekly plans for any skill  
- Includes:
  - Mini-topics  
  - Weekly projects  
  - Step-by-step progression  

---

### 🔗 Real Resource Integration
- Live search using **SerpAPI (Google Search)**  
- Provides:
  - YouTube tutorials  
  - Articles  
  - Courses  
- Avoids hallucinated links  

---

### 🧩 Interactive Learning
- Progress tracking with completion checkboxes  
- Built-in quizzes for each topic  
- Deep dives into advanced concepts  

---

### 💬 AI Guidance
- Ask questions directly within each topic  
- Get contextual explanations, resources, and next steps  
- Designed for users unfamiliar with AI tools  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend/UI:** Streamlit  
- **AI Generation:** Custom `gem3` client  
- **Search API:** SerpAPI (Google Search)  
- **State Management:** Streamlit Session State  
- **Environment Management:** python-dotenv
